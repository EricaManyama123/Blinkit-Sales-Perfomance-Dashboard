Blinkit Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI

Project Overview
This Power BI project presents a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit’s sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution.  
The goal was to identify key business insights and optimization opportunities through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.



Problem Statement
Blinkit required a clear, data-driven understanding of its sales trends, outlet performance, and customer feedback.  
The challenge was to transform raw transactional data into actionable insights that could support strategic decision-making on product demand, outlet operations, and inventory management.


Project Workflow
1. Requirement Gathering & Business Understanding 
   Defined objectives, KPIs, and visualization needs.  

2. Data Preparation 
   - Data collection and walkthrough  
   - Data cleaning and quality checks  
   - Data modeling and relationship building  

3. Data Processing & DAX Calculations
   Created calculated columns and measures for KPIs like revenue, average rating, and item count.  

4. Dashboard Design & Visualization 
   Designed an interactive layout highlighting key performance areas and insights.  

5. Insight Generation
   Analyzed visual trends to support business optimization and decision-making.



Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Total Sales – Overall revenue generated  
- Average Sales – Average revenue per transaction  
- Number of Items– Total count of unique products sold  
- Average Rating – Mean customer satisfaction score for sold items  


Charts & Visuals Used
| Metric | Visualization Type | Description |
|--------|--------------------|--------------|
| Total Sales by Fat Content | Donut Chart | Showed contribution of various fat categories to total revenue |
| Total Sales by Item Type | Bar Chart | Compared performance across different item categories |
| Fat Content by Outlet | Stacked Column Chart | Highlighted sales mix by outlet and fat content |
| Total Sales by Outlet Establishment | Line Chart | Displayed sales trends by year of establishment |
| Sales by Outlet Size | Donut/Area Chart | Illustrated revenue distribution across outlet sizes |
| Sales by Outlet Location | Funnel/Map Chart | Showed sales concentration across different locations |
| All Metrics by Outlet Type | Matrix Card | Provided a consolidated view of KPIs by outlet type |



Insights Generated
- Identified high-performing outlet types ontributing most to overall revenue.  
- Revealed sales dependency  on fat content and item category distribution.  
- Highlighted location-based sales trends supporting better outlet planning.  
- Enhanced understanding of customer ratings and item performance for data-driven improvements.



 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI– Dashboard design, DAX calculations, and data visualization  
- Microsoft Excel – Data cleaning and preprocessing  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)– KPI computation and measure logic  



Outcome
Delivered an interactive Power BI dashboard that visualized sales performance and customer behavior,  
enabling Blinkit to make informed decisions on sales optimization, outlet performance, and inventory planning.
